## Rules

1. **Always confirm before making code changes.** Do NOT edit files without asking the user to confirm first. Describe the proposed change and wait for approval.
2. **Always propose best-practice solutions first.** When solving a problem, research and suggest the best practical approach, explain the reasoning, and wait for the user to confirm before implementing.
3. **Keep explanations simple and plain.** No jargon-heavy walls of text. Explain what the change does in 2-3 simple sentences. Use plain language, not engineering terminology.
